---
title: "⏯️ Playground+"
description: "A better way to prompt engineer"
---

Test your apps and compare variants side by side. You have controls over all
GPT-3 parameters and models in Playground+. Move from concept to working prompt
in minutes instead of days.

Use the variable fields to test multiple examples and make adjustments. You have
multiple options for your testing interface.

### Features

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Human Feedback">
  All completions can be rated in the UI. Get a sense for how well your app is
  performing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Logs">
  Detailed logs to help you find what's going wrong
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shared Sessions">
  Collaborate with your team and come back to your work whenever.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
